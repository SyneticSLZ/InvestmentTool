One Shot LoRA

Video to LoRA made easy - High-quality consistent AI models
OneShotLoRA turns videos into high-quality, realistic AI models with consistent outputs. No more messy datasets. Just upload or link a video, and get a fine-tuned LoRA model ready for art, VFX, and AI content creation.

Hey Product Hunt! 👋 We’re excited to introduce OneShotLoRA : The only service that lets you turn a video into a high-quality AI LoRA model. Simply upload a video or link to a publicly available one (YouTube, Google Drive, Dropbox), and we’ll extract the most common face on screen to create a consistent AI model for cosplay, fan art, content creation, and more. Why did we build this? Fine-tuning AI models with scattered images often leads to inconsistent results. OneShotLoRA solves this by using video as a source, ensuring that it captures all the details of a human face—facial structure, features, expressions, and lighting—to create realistic, high-quality, and consistent outputs. No more mismatched details or inaccurate AI generations! What makes us different? ✅ The only video-to-LoRA service ✅ Captures detailed facial structure & features for hyper-realistic results ✅ Supports your favorite base model (currently Hunyuan, Flux, and SDXL) ✅ Private & secure – No login required, no data stored or collected ✅ Works with both uploaded videos and public links ⭐️ Dedicated fan base on Discord [https://discord.gg/HMHkZEknw7] and Civit AI [https://civitai.com/tag/oneshotlora] ⏳ Typically around 1 hour for SDXL, 2 hours for Flux, and 4 hours for Hunyuan video – quality over speed! Who’s it for? 🎭 Cosplayers: AI-generated versions of yourself in any style 🎨 Artists & Fan Art Creators: Instantly create high-quality character models 📸 Bloggers & Content Creators: Personalize AI-generated content 🎮 Game & VFX Creators: Build consistent AI models for characters. We’d love to hear your thoughts! Try it out and let us know ❤️
